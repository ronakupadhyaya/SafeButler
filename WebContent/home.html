Using Oscar for Providers
Oscar for Providers is where you’ll find everything you need to work with Oscar. We built the site to simplify your team’s workflows so that you can focus on delivering great care to our members.
Signing up for Oscar for Providers
To Sign Up a New Organization:
  1. Go to
    provider.hioscar.com
  2. Under Need Online Access? click Sign up
  3. Enter your primary Tax ID number to get started
  4. From there, follow the step-by-step registration instructions to register your organization and create a user login for the primary administrator for the account*
To Sign Up as a New User for an Existing Account:
  1. Ask the administrator for your organization's account to invite you to join from their account (see "Inviting users to your organization")
  2. Once you have been invited by the administrator, check your email for instructions on how to set up your account and sign in
* Note: It may take a few days for an Oscar representative to verify your tax ID and approve the account
Inviting users to your organization (Organization and team managers only)
There are two ways to invite users to your organization, from the Organization page or the Team page. Organization managers (admins) can invite users from either page, while team managers can only invite users from the Teams page.
To invite a new user from the Organization page, follow these steps:
  1. Go to the Organization page by opening the menu in the lower lefthand corner of your screen, where you see your name, and clicking the Organization link.
  2. On the Organization page, click the Edit staff button.
  3. In the menu that appears, click Invite new staff to join. This will take you to the invite page.
  4. On the Invite page, enter the user’s name, email address, and select any teams they should join. (Don’t know? Don’t worry, you can always edit this information later.)
  5. When adding teams, be sure to set proper permissions for the user. This controls what information the user will be able to access in Oscar Care. To protect patient privacy, we recommended granting as few permissions as possible to users.
To invite a new user from a Team page, follow these steps:
  1. Go to a Team page by opening the menu in the lower lefthand corner of your screen, where you see your name, clicking the Teams link, then clicking on one of the teams in the list.
  2. On the Team page, click the Edit staff button.
  3. In the menu that appears, type the name of the user you’d like to add and click Invite new staff to join. This will take you to the Invite page.
  4. On the Invite page, enter the user’s name, email address, and select any teams they should join. (Don’t know? Don’t worry, you can always edit this information later.)
  5. When adding teams, be sure to set proper permissions for the user. This controls what information the user will be able to access in Oscar Care. To protect patient privacy, we recommended granting as few permissions as possible to users.
Approving users (Organization managers only)
Oscar for Providers was designed to give organizations full control of the users who access their data. When a new user tries to join your organization, follow these steps to approve or deny their request.
  1. When a new user tries to join your organization, you’ll receive an email.
  2. To approve or deny the user, go to the Organization page by clicking on the link in the email or opening the menu in the lower lefthand corner of your screen and clicking the Organization link.
  3. On the Organization page, you’ll see a list of users in your Organization. Click the row of the user you’d like to approve or deny (they will have the status “Requested”) to go to that user’s profile page.
  4. On the profile page, click Approve to approve a user or Deny if you don’t recognize the individual or wish to deny access for any other reason.
Creating a team (Organization managers only)
Oscar Care allows you to create teams to better organize and maintain users in your organization and the data they can see. After a team has been created, team managers can add existing users within your organization to their team, or even extend invitations to new users.
To create a team, follow these steps:
  1. Go to the Manage Teams page by logging in to Oscar Care, opening the menu in the lower lefthand corner of the screen, and clicking Teams .
  2. Click Create Team in the upper right of your screen.
  3. Add a team name and a description.
  4. In the Privacy and permissions section, select the TINs related to this team. This team’s list of TINs can be used to grant users on the team access to related claims or payment data.
  5. You can further limit what users on the team can see by selecting NPIs. Adding NPIs limits users to only seeing data related to specific doctors or groups.
  6. Next, select a user or users to serve as the manager of this team. As a manager, the user will be able to add or remove other users from the team, edit the permissions of other users, and even invite new users to your organization.
  7. Finally, click Save and your team will be created.
Updating your organization's information (Organization managers only)
Oscar Care is a great way to track your organization’s claims and payments. To help make Oscar Care as useful as possible for your organization, be sure to provide us with the full list of TINs your organization uses when doing business with Oscar.
You can update your organization’s information and add new teams on the Organization page. To do so, follow these steps:
  1. Go to the Organization page by opening the menu in the lower lefthand corner of the screen, and click Organization .
  2. Add TINs, update your team name, or add/remove managers for your organization. Note: Oscar must verify any new TINs you add to your organization. This can take 1-3 business days.
  3. Finally, click Save in the upper right corner to ensure your changes are saved.
Updating what users can see and do (Organization and team managers only)
Using permissions, Oscar for Providers allows managers to control what each team member in your organization can see, like claims or payments data. Managers can also promote other users to manager. To adjust a user’s permissions, follow these steps:
  1. Go to the user’s profile page by clicking their name on either the Organization or an individual team’s page.
  2. Scroll to the Teams and Permissions section.
  3. Add or remove teams until the user has the correct list.
  4. Check or uncheck boxes to grant or remove a given permission for the user for the related team.
  5. Once a user has the correct set of permissions for the correct set of teams, click the Save button in the upper right corner to save your changes.
Submitting electronic pre-authorization requests online
Verified users with the Member Search permission can submit new pre-authorization requests and view outstanding requests directly from their web account.
To submit a new online pre-authorization request (eligible services only):
  1. Go to the
    Authorizations
    tab from the main homepage or the Authorizations tab from an eligible member profile page.
  2. Click New authorization request on the top right of the page
  3. Follow the instructions to determine whether a service requires pre-authorization
  4. If a service is eligible for online pre-authorization request submission, click on Submit Pre-Authorization Request , complete the form, upload clinical records, and submit
Enabling ACH Payments (Organization managers only)
Organization admins with the Manage Payments permission can add, verify, and link bank accounts to start receiving payments electronically via ACH.
Adding a Bank Account :
  1. From the Organization tab, go to the Electronic payment section and select Manage Payments .
  2. Under Bank accounts , select Add bank account .
  3. Select the account type and enter the account holder's name, routing number, and account number.
  4. Click Save .
Verifying a Bank Account :
  1. In order to verify your bank account, we will deposit 2 small amounts in your bank account in 1-4 business days.
  2. Once you see the amounts deposited in your bank account, go back to
    Payment Settings
    and select your bank account under Bank account .
  3. Enter the 2 amounts and click Confirm to verify your account.
Linking a Bank Account :
  1. Once you have verified your bank account, you can now link your account to one or more TINs under Configure payment by TIN on the
    Payment Settings
    page.
  2. Confirm your new payment settings by clicking Save in the top right corner.
  3. You will now start receiving ACH payments to your selected bank account.
Note: Once you link a bank account to a TIN, you will no longer receive paper statements for payments. Instead, you may view statements electronically in the
Payments
tab.
[Beta Feature] Accessing the Member Health Information Dashboard
The Member Health Information Dashboard is a view of a member's health data that includes information from the member's claims, lab results, and member-reported information, and accessible via the View health information button on the top right of every member profile page you are authorized to view.
Access to this dashboard requires the View health information permission that can be granted to a user by an administrator.
Note: Data shown on this dashboard may be incomplete or suppressed.
